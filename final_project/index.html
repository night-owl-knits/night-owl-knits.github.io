About Me

My name is Andrea, and I'm addicted to yarn. I love to buy yarn, pet it, hug it, and craft with it.

I blame this addiction on the Queen of Crafts, Martha Stewart; for as long as I can remember, I've aspired to follow in her crafty footsteps. I'm an amateur knitter, crocheter, cook, and all-around crafter who loves creating things by hand—from homemade pickles to handknit scarves, I'll try making (almost) anything once. Follow along as I attempt to live a mostly homemade life—I'm not saying I plan to churn my own butter, but you never know ...
